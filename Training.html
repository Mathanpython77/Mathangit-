A Vivo phone is an Android smartphone from the Chinese tech company Vivo, known for its innovative mobile photography features, strong performance, and competitive pricing across various segments, from budget to flagship. Vivo phones often feature high-quality displays with high refresh rates, long-lasting batteries with fast charging, and advanced camera systems, sometimes co-engineered with brands like ZEISS. They run on FunTouch OS, vivo's customized version of Android, offering unique features and AI capabilities.
